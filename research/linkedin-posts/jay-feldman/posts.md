Cold email is the #1 way to get clients for pennies.

But most people have no idea how it works.

To fix that? (After making $15M+ with cold email)

Here's a breakdown of how to build your first email machine: 

1. Setup

To send emails you'll need mailboxes attached to a domain, which will use a server.

Buy secondary domains from GoDaddy & use EasyDmarc to configure DNS Records (proves ownership).

Get Google & Microsoft mailboxes and use a warming tool (Instantly, Warmy) to establish them.

You also must create & install a CTD from your sending tool.

2. Leads

You need a list of people who need/want what you sell.

The best way is with a database (like Apollo) with regularly updated emails.

Set filters to target your ICP then copy Apollo's URL into TrustedLeads.

Use MillionVerifer to check email quality (remove bads & put riskies in a separate campaign).

3. Copy

Everything else is worthless if your email copy doesn't get replies.

Asking a short question in the SL ("Got Ads?") gets it opened, but getting it read is another story.

You must explain your offer, credibility/social proof & the value of replying in under 6 lines.

Don't ask for a call, just to send over more information.

4. Sending

The final step is to get your emails in your prospect's inbox.

Use a tool (Instantly) to personalize the 1st line of the email for more replies.

Constantly split test your offer/CTA to increase results over time.

Use AI to manage a central Unibox for leads (don't forward to a mailbox).

- -

That's how you create a cold email machine from scratch.
