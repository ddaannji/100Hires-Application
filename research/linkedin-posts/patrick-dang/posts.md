How to build a B2B SaaS prospecting list step by step:

Step 1: Set the Scene — Define your ICP precisely. Not just "SaaS companies" but "Series A-B SaaS companies with 20-100 employees in the US selling to SMBs."

Step 2: Create an Ideal Customer Profile — Go through your best 10 current clients. What do they have in common? Funding stage, team size, tech stack, geography, growth signals.

Step 3: Build Your List — Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator with your ICP filters. Start with 200-500 contacts, not 5,000. Quality over volume every time.

Step 4: Write and Send — Cold email + LinkedIn outreach in parallel. Email for scale. LinkedIn for warmth and replies from people who don't respond to email.

The order matters. ICP first, list second, copy third. Most people start with copy and wonder why it doesn't work.

---

**Why relevant:** Sequential ICP-first framework for B2B SaaS prospecting — shows the correct order of operations (ICP → list → copy) that prevents the most common cold outreach mistake of sending great copy to the wrong people.
