How to cold email in 2024?

Do this:

1. Hook prospects from the 1st line. Let the reason “WHY” you contacted shine.
2. Put a new perspective on how they can solve their biggest priority right now.
3. Show the impact a similar company experienced after solving this problem.
4. Don’t push prospects for the meeting in your CTA, leave them a way out.
5. Personalise in the PS section, it's a cherry on top of the relevant email.

Mobile optimise your message + make it easy to read = get more replies.

Don’t start your cold email with “Hi, my name is…”

There’s a signature in your emails, people can see it. 

It’s not disrespectful to skip greetings. 

You'll build trust faster if you're going to get straight to the point.

The same goes for these:

“How are you doing?”
“We’re an award-winning…”
"I hope this email finds you well

All are a waste of space. 

Let other sellers focus on prospects' well-being (they get such emails every day)

You have a max of 60-70 words - use them wisely.

• Yurii - your CEO <CEO name> mentioned on a pod that pipeline optimization is the main goal for Q1.
• Hi, Matt - <colleague name> told me that…
• Artur - despite 193k visits in September, Woodpecker had only…

Direct to the point shows that you respect the prospect’s time. It gets their attention.

PS. Why be generic when you can stand out?

**Why relevant:** Shows exactly how to front-load cold email openers with research-based hooks — the first 18 words are the most valuable real estate in any cold email, and this post gives concrete rewrite examples.
